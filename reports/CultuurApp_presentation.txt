Suppose you are a tourist on a trip in the Netherlands. You're in a cultural mode and you want to visit some monuments.

With CultuurApp you can lookup and explore monuments in the Netherlands. 

- You can get look them up on a map
- Filter them on the list view
- Quickly find new monuments by browsing the tags
- View individual monument and see
	- their full description from Cultural Heritage Agency
	- their location and all kinds of meta data
- You can also view the weather information around the monument
- Find restaurants and bars in the neighbourhood
- Locate the venue on FourSquare and check in

We also recommend monuments that are visited by users like you. We can compare monuments based on their description and their visual information.

We have completed the dataset with tags and categories. The predicting of the category works really well, we got a success ratio of 86 percent.
